## Key Concepts Learned

### Port Forwarding
- Allows external traffic from the internet to reach a specific device inside a local network
- Uses ports to direct traffic to the correct internal device

Example:
Internet → Router → Local Device

---

### Firewalls
- Firewalls filter and control network traffic
- Can allow or block connections based on rules
- Operate mainly at OSI Layers 3 and 4

---

### VPN Basics
- VPN creates an encrypted tunnel between a device and the internet
- Helps protect privacy and secure traffic on public networks

---
